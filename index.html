Welcome to the land of Sends and data analysis

Here you can use all you previous performances in order to tailor you training planning, inform decisions about your next trip or just show off to your friends.
The main tab you'll be interested in is the Dashboard.

The Dashboard:

The Dashboard shows a serie of graph ranging from the classical grades pyramid to the max, min and average number of tries per grade.
On this tab you can edit the filters of the second row (Climber, Grade, Crag, etc.)
